Client Experience

At Bailey Lauerman we’re passionate about creating smart, respectful, and clever work.

Bosch Invented for Life

In the summer of 2017 we won Bosch Icon as a client and created the “Calm in the Storm” campaign. Since that partnership began we’ve won their international wiper business. Recently we have been invited to pitch for other sectors of the brand both nationally and internationally. Why? Client satisfaction leading to referrals.

Community America Banking on Human Connection

In one of the most crowded financial markets in the US, we needed to help our clients stand out. With a market outspending us by 10:1, we decided to redefine wealth. Customers told us what brings wealth to their lives and we turned their responses into a powerful campaign, reminding people of the true riches in life.

CUTIES 100 days of sunshine

Cuties clementine’s are a delicious snack for children and adults alike. They’re grown with love in California, and taste best from November to March. During peak season, we created 100 days of sunshine encouraging consumers to share a little love during the winter months. With help from our friends at iHeartRadio: Ryan Seacrest, Mario Lopez, Halsey, and Charlie Puth, we spread love around the country.

Lincoln Children’s Zoo Zooglies

We helped unleash children’s imaginations at the Lincoln zoo by creating our own creatures we called Zooglies. Zooglies helped set an all-time attendance record for our clients at the Lincoln Zoo, with more than 191,000 visitors. This surpassed the previous year by 18% and increased summer camp revenue by 58%.

Nebraska Tourism Commission Visit Nebraska. Visit Nice.

We created a series where locals showed visitors what Nebraska looks like to them with the “Through My Eyes” campaign. No logos. No headlines. Just authentic story telling. We increased site traffic from social media by 668% and unique visitors to the VisitNebraska.com site increased by 125%.

Panda Express Chinese Kitchen

Panda Express is in a cuisine category of its own: American Chinese. Since 2012 we helped define the brand and create space for it in the fast dinning category. We brought seasonal dishes to life through several mediums, from tv to packaging. We also brought the national spotlight to our clients by branding their own eating utensil called it the Chork.
