Adding a Marketer

To add a Marketer, first you want to log into the Phillips 66/Kendall CMS. When you're in, do the following:

  1. Navigate to the marketers section by clicking the Marketer button and then clicking Add Marketer.
  2. Once there, add the name, country, and company you wish for the marketer's information to be stored in:

  1. Click Save, and you'll be taken to a secondary detail screen for your Marketer. From here you want to enter as much information as possible.
    *When it comes to the latitude and longitute, I found it is easiest to use an online service such as latlong.net when the data is not supplied.

  1. Once you are done, click Save Marketer, and you're finished!

Editing an Existing Marketer

To edit an existing Marketer, you will first want to log into the Phillips 66/Kendall CMS. Once in:

  1. Navigate to the Marketers section by clicking the Marketer button.
  2. From there, search for your Marketer by name within the Search Criteria Box.
    Be sure that you're searching within the correct brand.

  1. When you find the Marketer you wish to edit, simply click the pencil icon, , to the left of that Marketer.
  2. This will bring up the Marketer detail screen. From here, update the stored information and click Save Marketer, and you're finished!

Adding a Product

To add a product, first you want to log into the Phillips 66/Kendall CMS. Once in:

  1. Navigate to the Product section by clicking the Product Inventory button and then clicking Add New Product.
  2. Once there, add the product name, slug (URL), and brand you wish for the product information to be stored in. Then click Save.

  1. This will bring up the product detail screen. From here, you must update the information in each of the product tabs.

  1. Description
    • Headline - This will be the headline displayed in the hero area of the page.
    • Sub Header - This is the sub header text displayed in the right margin of the page.
    • SDS Number - Adding the SDS number will automatically add a link to the safety data sheet.
    • Content - This information can be taken from the first paragraph of the data sheet. Please remember to wrap the text in paragraph tags.
      *The addition of a content editor is an enhancement that we have scoped out for later delivery
